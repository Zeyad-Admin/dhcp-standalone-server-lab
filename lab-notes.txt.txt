DHCP Standalone Server Lab

Environment:
Domain: corp.mog
DC: DC-Z (192.168.50.10)
DHCP Server: DHCP-Z (192.168.50.20)
Client: CL-Z1

Objective:
Move DHCP service from the domain controller to a dedicated DHCP server.

Steps Completed:
1. Built DHCP-Z server
2. Joined DHCP-Z to domain
3. Installed DHCP role
4. Authorized DHCP in AD
5. Exported DHCP from DC-Z
6. Imported DHCP to DHCP-Z
7. Disabled DHCP on DC-Z
8. Verified client receives IP from DHCP-Z
